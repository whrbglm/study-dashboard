  • Apr 27 — Korean IO 🎤 (10-min speech)

  • May 5 — Chemistry HL exam ⚗
  • Three major events in 8 days — plan each day carefully
  • Korean IO drops after Apr 27 → use that slot for Bio + Chem crunch

⚗ Chem Exam — May 5

  • Structure 1 — Atomic structure, periodicity, ideal gases, equilibrium, thermochem, radioactivity
  • Structure 2 — Ionic/covalent/metallic bonding, VSEPR, IMF, acids & bases, Gibbs energy
  • Structure 3 — Functional groups, organic reactions, mechanisms, isomerism, electrochemistry, kinetics
  • Reactivity 1 — Collision theory, rate laws, activation energy, catalysts, reaction mechanisms
  • Prioritise data booklet navigation — know where everything is
  • Past papers: Structure Qs appear every year — do them timed

🔥 Semester Test (End of May)

  • Most important test — treat it like final exams
  • Math AA HL: Functions, Trig, Calc, Diff, Integration
  • Bio HL: Study guide + full past papers (P1/P2/P3)
  • Chem HL: Structure 1–3 + Reactivity 1 · past papers · data booklet
  • Econ SL: Micro + Macro + Intro · DEED essays · diagrams
  • Spaced repetition past papers accelerate from Week 5
